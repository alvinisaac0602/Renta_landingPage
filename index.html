Safety Guard

How Renta Secures Your Search

We built Renta to completely eliminate fake brokers, double-letting scams, and lock-out risks — through four layers of protection.

NIN Vetting System

Every landlord is verified against the National Identification (NIRA) database before being allowed to list any spaces.

EXIF Vetting Checks

We scan photo metadata and use image-matching models to ensure listings feature genuine on-site photos, preventing duplicate scams.

Secure Escrow Holding

Your rent deposit remains locked in secure escrow and is only released to the landlord once you confirm physical handover on-site.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about setting up and using the Renta app in Uganda.

How does Renta protect tenants from double-listing fraud?

Every landlord must submit valid biometric IDs matched against NIRA records. Properties pass geographic metadata checks. Your deposit is locked in Renta Escrow until you physically verify the property and confirm key handover.

Is the Renta app free to download and use?

Yes. Downloading Renta, exploring listings, booking viewing tours, and direct chat are completely free. Only when you execute a contract is a small flat fee of 3% applied on the deposit — clearly disclosed before you proceed.

What happens if the space is not as described during viewing?

Simply cancel the request in-app. Since your deposit has not been released from escrow, funds are instantly refunded to your Renta wallet or mobile money number — no questions asked.

Can I pay rent via Mobile Money on Renta?

Absolutely. Renta features MTN Mobile Money and Airtel Money integrations. Pay deposits, monthly rent, and movers fees from your mobile wallet — with digital receipts automatically logged.

Which cities in Uganda does Renta cover?

Renta currently covers Kampala, Entebbe, Wakiso, Mukono, Jinja, Mbarara, Gulu, Lira, and 7 more cities — with new locations launching quarterly. You'll see a "Coming Soon" badge for cities being onboarded.

How do I list my property on Renta as a landlord?

Download the Renta app, create a Host account, submit your National ID for NIN verification, then upload property photos with accurate location metadata. Our moderation team reviews and approves listings within 48 hours.
