Safety Guard

How Renta Secures Your Search

We built Renta to completely eliminate fake brokers, double-letting scams, and lock-out risks.

NIN Vetting System

Every landlord or rental manager is verified against the National Identification database (NIRA) before being allowed to list any spaces.

EXIF Vetting Checks

We scan photo upload properties metadata and leverage image-matching models to ensure listings feature genuine on-site photos, preventing duplicate scams.

Secure Escrow Holding

Your rent deposit remains locked in secure escrow and is only released to the landlord once you confirm physical handover and keys on-site.

Tenant Review Ledger

Read honest, immutable reviews from past tenants about landlords, water pressure, utility costs, and neighborhood security scores.

Help Desk

Frequently Answered Questions

Everything you need to know about setting up and using the Renta app in Uganda.

How does Renta protect tenants from double-listing fraud?

Every landlord registering on Renta must submit valid biometric IDs matched against National database records. Properties must also pass geographic metadata checks. Crucially, your rent deposit is locked in Renta Escrow until you physically verify the property on-site and confirm handover.

Is the Renta app free to download and use?

Yes. Downloading Renta, exploring listing streams, booking physical viewing tours, and direct chat are completely free. If you select a space and execute a contract, a small flat fee of 3% is applied on deposits handling and smart lease execution.

What happens if the space is not as described during viewing?

If you perform a viewing tour and decide not to proceed, simply cancel the request in the app. Because your deposit has not been released from our escrow system, the funds are instantly refunded to your Renta wallet or mobile money number.

Can I pay rent via Mobile Money on Renta?

Absolutely. Renta features MTN Mobile Money and Airtel Money integrations. You can pay security deposits, monthly rent, and movers fees directly from your mobile wallet, generating digital receipts automatically logged in the ledger.
